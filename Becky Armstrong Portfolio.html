About Me


Hey there, my name is Becky and I'm an Instructional Designer and LMS administrator based in Denver, Colorado. I specialize in healthcare training and compliance, and have worked in both mental and physical health care since 2010. My job is to create dynamic trainings that grab the attention of your employees and give them the skills necessary to excel. I specialize in module animation and graphic design, and customized interactivity. If you're interested in a free consult, fill out my contact form.
